Understanding HTML Headings and Paragraphs

Welcome to the section on HTML headings and paragraphs. In this part of the course, you'll learn about the importance of headings and paragraphs in structuring your HTML documents.

Headings

Headings are essential for organizing your content hierarchically. They range from <h1> to <h6>, with <h1> being the most important and <h6> being the least. Here's an example:

<h1>Main Heading</h1>
        <h2>Subheading</h2>
        ...
        <h6>Minor Heading</h6>

Paragraphs

Paragraphs are used to group related sentences or content together. They provide structure and improve readability. Here's how you create a paragraph in HTML:

<p>This is a paragraph.</p>
